Automn

Overview

I was as a UI/UX designer for Automn, a student led startup. Autumn is a productivity and scheduling app to help students and young professionals spend their time wisely to achieve their goals. Automn allows users to import their calendar into the app and input what projects and goals they want to work on. From there, Automn helps them schedule their free time to work towards their goals. I worked with five other teammates to create an improved information architecture, design library, and prototype for Automn.

The Problem

The pre-existing information architecture was broad and unfocused. Our clients were aiming to create a habit tracker, planner, calendar, and a social app, all with an artificial intelligence chatbot. It wasn’t clear what problem the app was trying to solve. Furthermore, the visual design was cluttered and unappealing to an audience of young professionals.

Goals

- Conduct user research to identify a more specific use case of the app, and redesign the information architecture around it.
- Create a contemporary design library that remains on brand to the “Automn” theme.

User Research

We conducted 12 long-form interviews with potential users to learn about their productivity and scheduling habits. We also provided a short questionnaire for other potential users to evaluate the pre-existing screens and information architecture. From there, we analyzed our results to draw insights about our users.
