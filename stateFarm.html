Storytelling in VR

During the summer of 2020, I interned at the State Farm Research and Development Center, working in a team to design and implement an interactive virtual reality experience based off of an existing State Farm commerical. I created original 3D assets using Blender and coded the experience using Unity. I am unable to display my work publicly, but please feel free to reach out via email or LinkedIn if you would like to see my work.
